# FreePeriod — Product Context

## Register

Brand-led product. The public site should feel warm, teacher-friendly, and trustworthy — not like a generic AI SaaS landing page.

## Users

Teachers planning lessons under time pressure. They need structured output quickly, with room to edit before exporting.

## Product Purpose

Generate structured lesson plans from curriculum context and a short brief. Plans are editable and exportable (DOCX, filled templates).

## Brand Personality

- Warm and approachable
- Efficient — speed without sacrificing structure
- Trustworthy — clear outputs teachers can use in class
- Teacher-friendly — plain language, no hype

## Anti-References

- Generic AI SaaS aesthetics (purple gradients, glassmorphism overload)
- Over-animated startup pages that compete with the message
- Cold enterprise dashboards

## Accessibility

- WCAG AA contrast targets
- Visible focus states (coral ring)
- Reduced-motion support for all entrance and ambient animations
- Minimum 44px touch targets on interactive controls

## Visual Direction

- Typography and spacing carry hierarchy — effects support, never lead
- Coral + mustard palette with warm white background
- Coloured pictogram as primary mark
- Gentle motion (aurora, pictogram breathe, section reveals)
